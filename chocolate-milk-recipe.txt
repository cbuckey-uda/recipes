Chocolate Milk Recipe:

Ingredients and Supplies Needed:
	Milk
	Cup
	Spoon
	Chocolate Syrup
	Cookies (optional)


Steps:
	1) Pour milk into a cup by tilting it in a horizontal fashion
	2) Stop pouring when milk nearly reaches the brim
	3) Pour chocolate syrup into the cup of milk (some prefer to start with this step)
	4) No one is looking, go ahead and pour some more syrup
	5) Grab that spoon and stir. Your goal here is to recreate the movie Twister in your cup
	6) When the whiteness of the milk has become a brown, you can stop stirring
	7) Drink or dip cookies
